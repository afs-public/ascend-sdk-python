Indicates how often the asset is valued. Critical for pricing workflows, client expectations, and operational scheduling.

| `SEMIMONTHLY`                     | SEMIMONTHLY                       |
| `QUARTERLY`                       | QUARTERLY                         |
| `ANNUALLY`                        | ANNUALLY                          |
| `OTHER`                           | OTHER                             |
